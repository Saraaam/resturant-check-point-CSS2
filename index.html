À propos de nous

The River Cafe has been, and still is, the most exclusive fine dining restaurant in Morocco since 1999. Located in one of casa’s most noteworthy and romantic settings, our American restaurant is well known around the world as a wonderful culinary destination. Nestled riverside under the Brooklyn Bridge, The River Café exudes romance with its breathtaking casa’s skyline views, gorgeous atmosphere and above all our classic American cuisine. The River Café similarly boasts one of the most outstanding wine lists in the world.
